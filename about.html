Our Story

A little story, softly told.

GlowKatha is a small, woman-led gifting studio born out of a love for slow mornings, warm light and the quiet joy of a well-wrapped box.

Every candle carries a little story.

What began as a hobby — pouring candles for friends in tiny blush jars — slowly grew into a little brand built on care.

Today, GlowKatha crafts premium candles, wax melts, sachets and heartfelt return favours for weddings, baby showers and everyday celebrations.
