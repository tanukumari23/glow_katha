Our Story

A little story, softly told.

GlowKatha is a small, woman-led gifting studio born out of a love for slow mornings, warm light and the quiet joy of a well-wrapped box.

Every candle carries a little story.

What started as a simple passion for creating aesthetic, comforting candles slowly became a journey of celebrating emotions, gifting, homes, and special occasions.

Every candle is thoughtfully handcrafted to bring a sense of calm, elegance, and joy into your space. From cozy home décor pieces to meaningful gifts for baby showers, weddings, festive celebrations, and corporate hampers — each creation is designed to feel personal and memorable.
